Shipping Policy

Shipping Rates:

70/- flat shipping charge on orders below or equal to 700/-

Free Shipping on order above 700/-

 

Order Processing:

We strive to fulfill orders as soon as you place them. In most cases, your order will be sent to our delivery partner within 1-2 business days. Our business days are Monday-Friday.

 

Shipping Time:

For most serviceable pin codes, we try to deliver within 7 days. But due to unforeseen circumstances like weather, strikes, remote locations, stocking issues, or any other reason - it may take longer. You will however be able to track your package using a unique tracking link that we will email/SMS you after your order is sent to our delivery partner.

 

Problem with your delivery?

In case of any problem or changes to your delivery please contact us , our support team will resolve your query within 24 hours Guaranteed!
